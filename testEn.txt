From the AP comes this story : President Bush on Tuesday nominated two
individuals to replace retiring jurists on federal courts in the Washington
area. Bush nominated Jennifer M. Anderson for a 15-year term as associate judge
of the Superior Court of the District of Columbia, replacing Steffen W. Graae.
*** Bush also nominated A. Noel Anketell Kramer for a 15-year term as associate
judge of the District of Columbia Court of Appeals, replacing John Montague
Steadman.
